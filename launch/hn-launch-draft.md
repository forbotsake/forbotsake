Show HN: ForBotSake, an open-source GTM workflow for technical founders

## Post body draft
I kept seeing the same pattern with technical founders:

They could ship the product.
They could not explain it cleanly, target the right buyer, or build a repeatable distribution system.

Most AI marketing tools start too late. They help you write a tweet, blog post, or launch thread after the strategic confusion is already baked in.

So I built ForBotSake.

It is an open-source GTM workflow for technical founders. You point it at a product/repo and it helps produce:
- positioning
- ICP research
- competitor analysis
- content plans
- launch assets
- outreach workflows

The point is not more AI copy.
The point is more GTM clarity.

I wanted it to feel more like a marketing operating system than a prompt pack.

Repo: https://github.com/forbotsake/forbotsake

Would especially love feedback from:
- devtool founders
- OSS maintainers trying to commercialize
- builders who shipped something real but still feel fuzzy on distribution
