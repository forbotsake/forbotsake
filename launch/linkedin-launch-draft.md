# LinkedIn Launch Post Draft

A lot of technical founders do not have a product problem anymore.

They have a translation problem.

They can build fast.
They cannot always explain:
- what the product really is
- who it is for
- why it matters now
- what to say first
- where it should go

That is the gap ForBotSake is built for.

I just open-sourced it.

ForBotSake is a GTM workflow for technical founders. It turns a repo, a rough launch story, and founder context into:
- sharper positioning
- clearer ICP
- competitor contrast
- content and launch assets
- outreach plans
- repeatable distribution workflows

In plain English:
You built the product.
Now build the go-to-market system.

If you are a devtool founder, OSS maintainer, or technical builder with real product but weak traction, I would love your read on it.
